### Baked Tilapia with Veggies
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Tilapia fillets  
- Broccoli  
- Lemon  
- Garlic  
- Olive oil  
- Salt and pepper  

**Quick Prep:**  
Arrange tilapia and broccoli on a sheet pan, drizzle with olive oil, garlic, and lemon, then bake at 400°F for 15 minutes.

**Tags:** #fish #american #healthy #30min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 25 min
