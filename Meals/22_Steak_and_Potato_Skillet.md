### Steak and Potato Skillet
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Steak  
- Potatoes  
- Onion  
- Olive oil or butter  
- Garlic  
- Paprika or steak seasoning  

**Quick Prep:**  
Pan-fry potatoes and onions. Add steak and cook to desired doneness.

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 25 min
