### Tomato Cream Shrimp Campanelle
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Shrimp  
- Campanelle or shell pasta  
- Tomato paste  
- Garlic  
- Broccoli or broccolini  
- Cream  
- Parmesan cheese  
- Red pepper flakes  

**Quick Prep:**  
Cook pasta and broccoli. Sauté shrimp and garlic, stir in tomato cream sauce. Toss everything together.

**Tags:** #shrimp #pasta #creamy #spicy #35min

**Prep Time:** 15 min | **Total Time:** 35 min
