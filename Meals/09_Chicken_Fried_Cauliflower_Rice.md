### Chicken Fried Cauliflower Rice
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Chicken breast  
- Cauliflower rice  
- Frozen stir fry vegetables  
- Eggs  
- Soy sauce  
- Garlic or green onion  

**Quick Prep:**  
Scramble egg, cook chicken and veg, mix with cauliflower rice and soy sauce.

**Tags:** #chicken #lowcarb #onepan #35min

**Prep Time:** 15 min | **Total Time:** 35 min
