### BLT Sandwiches
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Bacon  
- Bread  
- Lettuce  
- Tomato  
- Mayonnaise  

**Quick Prep:**  
Cook bacon until crispy, toast the bread, then layer with mayo, lettuce, tomato, and bacon.

**Tags:** #pork #american #quick #15min

**Prep Time:** 5 min | **Total Time:** 15 min
