### Taco Chicken Frito Bowls
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Chicken or pork  
- Taco seasoning  
- Canned beans  
- Canned corn  
- Cheese  
- Fritos or tortilla chips  
- Sour cream (optional)  

**Quick Prep:**  
Warm everything together. Serve over chips with cheese and toppings.

**Tags:** #chicken #pork #bowl #usescans #20min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 20 min
