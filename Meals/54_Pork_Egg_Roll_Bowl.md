### Pork Egg Roll in a Bowl
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Ground pork  
- Slaw mix or cabbage + carrots  
- Green onions  
- Soy sauce  
- Sweet & sour or plum sauce  
- Dijon or dry mustard  
- Peanuts  
- Crispy wonton strips  

**Quick Prep:**  
Cook pork, add sauces and slaw. Top with peanuts and wontons.

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 20 min
