### Southwest Bowl
**Main Ingredients:**
- Chicken Breast
- Black Beans (canned)
- Diced Tomatoes (canned)
- Cream of Chicken Soup (canned)
- Corn (canned)
- Cooked rice
- Milk

**Quick Prep:**
Brown chicken on stovetop, mix canned goods with cream of chicken and milk, serve over rice.

**Tags:** #chicken #american #30min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 30 min

**Instructions:**
**Step 1**
Cook 4 cups of rice according to package directions.

**Step 2**
Season and brown chicken in a skillet over medium-high heat, about 5-6 minutes per side. Remove and slice or cube.

**Step 3**
In the same skillet, combine 1 can black beans (drained and rinsed), 1 can diced tomatoes, half a can of corn (drained), 1 can cream of chicken soup, and a quarter cup of milk. Stir and heat through over medium heat, about 5 minutes.

**Step 4**
Add chicken back to the skillet and stir to combine. Serve over rice.
