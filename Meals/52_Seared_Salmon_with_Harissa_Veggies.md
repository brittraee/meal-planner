### Seared Salmon with Harissa Vegetables
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Salmon fillet  
- Potatoes  
- Carrots  
- Red onion  
- Cabbage  
- Dried dates  
- Harissa paste  
- White wine vinegar  

**Quick Prep:**  
Roast veggies and cabbage with harissa. Sear salmon. Top with red onion-date vinaigrette.

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 30 min
