### Skillet Chili with Meatballs
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Cooked meatballs  
- Canned beans  
- Diced tomatoes  
- Onion  
- Chili seasoning  
- Shredded cheese (optional)  

**Quick Prep:**  
Slice meatballs and simmer with beans, tomatoes, and seasoning. Serve in bowls or over rice.

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 25 min
