### Steak Quesadillas
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Steak  
- Tortillas  
- Shredded cheese  
- Onion or peppers  
- Salsa or sour cream (optional)  

**Quick Prep:**  
Cook steak, slice thin. Fill tortillas with steak, cheese, and veg. Toast until golden.

**Tags:** #steak #quesadilla #quickmeal #20min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 20 min
