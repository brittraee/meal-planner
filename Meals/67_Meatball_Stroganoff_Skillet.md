### Meatball Stroganoff Skillet
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Meatballs  
- Egg noodles or pasta  
- Sour cream  
- Onion or mushroom  
- Beef broth or bouillon  

**Quick Prep:**  
Simmer meatballs in creamy broth with noodles and onion. Stir in sour cream.

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 25 min
