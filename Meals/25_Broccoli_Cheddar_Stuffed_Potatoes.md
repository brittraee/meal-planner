### Broccoli Cheddar Stuffed Potatoes
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Russet or baking potatoes  
- Broccoli  
- Shredded cheddar  
- Butter or milk  
- Sour cream (optional)  

**Quick Prep:**  
Bake or microwave potatoes. Mix broccoli and cheese into scooped potato filling. Refill and broil.

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 25 min
