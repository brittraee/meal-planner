### Chicken Quesadillas
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Chicken breast  
- Flour tortillas  
- Shredded cheese  
- Bell pepper  
- Onion  
- Optional: sour cream or salsa  

**Quick Prep:**  
Cook and dice chicken, fill tortillas with chicken, cheese, peppers, and onion, then pan-crisp until golden on each side.

**Tags:** #chicken #mexican #quick #kidfriendly #20min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 20 min
