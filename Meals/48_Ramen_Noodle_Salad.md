### Ramen Noodle Salad (Side Dish)
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Instant ramen (uncooked)  
- Coleslaw mix  
- Sliced almonds or sunflower seeds  
- Green onions  
- Sesame dressing or homemade vinaigrette  

**Quick Prep:**  
Crush ramen and mix with slaw and toppings. Toss with dressing.

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 15 min
