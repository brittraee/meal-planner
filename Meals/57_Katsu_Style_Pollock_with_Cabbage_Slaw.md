### Katsu-Style Wild Alaskan Pollock
**Main Ingredients:**  
- White fish (pollock or cod)  
- Panko breadcrumbs  
- Egg  
- Flour  
- Jasmine rice  
- Cabbage  
- Scallions  
- Mayo + rice vinegar + sweet chili sauce  

**Quick Prep:**  
Bread and fry fish katsu-style. Mix slaw with dressing. Serve with rice and drizzle with sauce.

**Tags:** #katsu #fish #asianfusion #crispy #35min

**Prep Time:** 15 min | **Total Time:** 35 min
