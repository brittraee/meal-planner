### Grilled Cheese and Tomato Soup
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Bread  
- Butter  
- American or cheddar cheese  
- Canned crushed tomatoes  
- Onion  
- Garlic  
- Chicken or vegetable broth  

**Quick Prep:**  
Simmer tomatoes with onion, garlic, and broth then blend smooth while grilling buttered cheese sandwiches in a skillet.

**Tags:** #vegetarian #american #comfortfood #kidfriendly #30min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 30 min
