### Garlic Chicken Grilled Cheese
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Chicken breast  
- White bread or sourdough  
- Butter  
- Garlic  
- Shredded cheese or cheese slices  

**Quick Prep:**  
Mix cooked chicken with garlic and cheese. Grill inside buttered bread until golden.

**Tags:** #chicken #grilledcheese #comfortfood #kidfriendly #20min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 20 min
