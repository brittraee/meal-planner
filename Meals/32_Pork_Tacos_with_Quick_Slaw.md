### Pork Tacos with Quick Slaw
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Shredded pork  
- Taco seasoning  
- Tortillas  
- Coleslaw mix  
- Lime or vinegar  
- Sour cream or salsa (optional)  

**Quick Prep:**  
Toss pork with seasoning. Warm in skillet. Serve in tortillas with slaw + toppings.

**Tags:** #pork #tacos #quickmeal #familyfriendly #20min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 20 min
