### Turkey Burgers
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Ground turkey  
- Hamburger buns  
- Lettuce  
- Tomato  
- Onion  
- Worcestershire sauce  
- Optional: avocado  

**Quick Prep:**  
Mix turkey with Worcestershire and seasoning, form patties, and grill or pan-sear about 5 minutes per side until cooked through.

**Tags:** #unknown #american #healthy #30min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 25 min
