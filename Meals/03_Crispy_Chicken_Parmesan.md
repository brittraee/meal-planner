### Crispy Chicken Parmesan
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Chicken breast  
- Panko breadcrumbs  
- Parmesan cheese  
- Pasta  
- Marinara or pasta sauce  
- Mozzarella or Italian blend cheese  

**Quick Prep:**  
Bread chicken with panko and Parmesan. Bake, top with sauce and cheese, then broil.

**Tags:** #chicken #bake #comfortfood #40min

**Prep Time:** 15 min | **Total Time:** 40 min
