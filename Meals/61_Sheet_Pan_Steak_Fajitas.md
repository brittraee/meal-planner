### Sheet Pan Steak Fajitas
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Steak strips  
- Bell peppers  
- Onion  
- Fajita seasoning  
- Flour tortillas  

**Quick Prep:**  
Toss steak and veg with seasoning. Roast on sheet pan. Serve in tortillas.

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 30 min
