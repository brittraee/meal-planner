### Baked Ziti
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Ziti pasta  
- Ground beef  
- Marinara sauce  
- Ricotta cheese  
- Mozzarella cheese  
- Parmesan cheese  
- Garlic  

**Quick Prep:**  
Brown beef with garlic, layer cooked ziti with meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella, then bake at 375°F for 25 minutes.

**Tags:** #beef #italian #batchcook #comfortfood #60min

**Prep Time:** 20 min | **Total Time:** 55 min
