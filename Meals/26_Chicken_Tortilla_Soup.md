### Chicken Tortilla Soup
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Cooked chicken  
- Canned tomatoes  
- Canned beans  
- Corn  
- Taco seasoning  
- Tortilla strips or chips  

**Quick Prep:**  
Combine everything in a pot. Simmer until heated through. Top with chips and cheese.

**Tags:** #chicken #soup #comfortfood #batchcook #30min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 30 min
