### Spinach Artichoke Chicken Bake
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Chicken breast  
- Cream cheese  
- Frozen spinach  
- Canned artichokes  
- Parmesan or mozzarella cheese  
- Garlic  

**Quick Prep:**  
Mix cream cheese, garlic, spinach, and artichokes. Spread over chicken. Top with cheese and bake.

**Tags:** #chicken #bake #comfortfood #40min

**Prep Time:** 15 min | **Total Time:** 40 min
