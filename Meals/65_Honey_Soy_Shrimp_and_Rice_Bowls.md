### Honey Soy Shrimp & Rice Bowls
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Shrimp  
- Honey  
- Soy sauce  
- Garlic or ginger  
- Jasmine rice  
- Frozen mixed veg or broccoli  

**Quick Prep:**  
Pan-cook shrimp in honey soy glaze. Serve over rice with steamed veggies.

**Tags:** #shrimp #ricebowl #asianfusion #batchfriendly #20min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 20 min
