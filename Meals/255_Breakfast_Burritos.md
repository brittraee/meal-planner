### Breakfast Burritos
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Eggs  
- Breakfast sausage  
- Flour tortillas  
- Shredded cheese  
- Bell pepper  
- Optional: salsa  

**Quick Prep:**  
Brown sausage, scramble eggs with peppers and cheese, then wrap everything in warm tortillas.

**Tags:** #pork #american #breakfastfordinner #30min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 25 min
