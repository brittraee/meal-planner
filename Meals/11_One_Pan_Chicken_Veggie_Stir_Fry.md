### One-Pan Chicken & Veggie Stir Fry
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Chicken breast  
- Frozen stir fry vegetables  
- Garlic  
- Soy sauce  
- Jasmine rice  
- Sesame oil (optional)  

**Quick Prep:**  
Cook chicken, add garlic and vegetables, stir in soy sauce. Serve over rice.

**Tags:** #chicken #onepan #ricebowl #quick #25min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 25 min
