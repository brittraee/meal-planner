### Chicken Cauliflower Rice Bowl
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Chicken breast  
- Cauliflower rice  
- Stir fry vegetables  
- Garlic  
- Soy sauce  
- Egg (optional)  

**Quick Prep:**  
Cook chicken, scramble egg, stir-fry veg and rice with soy sauce.

**Tags:** #chicken #lowcarb #onepan #quick #20min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 20 min
