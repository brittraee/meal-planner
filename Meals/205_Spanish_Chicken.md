### Spanish Chicken
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Chicken Thighs  
- Onion  
- Paprika  
- Lemon  
- Parsley  
- Chicken Stock  
- Olive Oil  

**Quick Prep:**  
Heat oven to 190C/170C fan/gas 5. Put all the ingredients into a large, wide ovenproof dish.

**Tags:** #chicken #spanish #55min

**Prep Time:** 14 min | **Total Time:** 54 min

**Instructions:**  
**Step 1**
Heat oven to 190C/170C fan/gas 5. Put all the ingredients into a large, wide ovenproof dish. Mix everything together with your hands and season.

**Step 2**
Bake for 45 mins, stirring the onions after 20 mins, until the chicken is golden and the onions tender. Serve with rice.
