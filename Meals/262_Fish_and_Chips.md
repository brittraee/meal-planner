### Fish and Chips
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Cod fillets  
- Flour  
- Eggs  
- Panko breadcrumbs  
- Potatoes  
- Vegetable oil  
- Optional: malt vinegar  

**Quick Prep:**  
Cut potatoes into fries and bake at 425°F, then bread cod in flour-egg-panko and fry until golden and flaky.

**Tags:** #fish #british #comfortfood #45min

**Prep Time:** 15 min | **Total Time:** 45 min
