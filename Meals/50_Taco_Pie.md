### Taco Pie
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Ground beef  
- Taco seasoning  
- Crescent rolls or pie crust  
- Refried beans  
- Cheese  
- Sour cream or salsa  

**Quick Prep:**  
Press crust into pie pan. Layer beans, meat, and cheese. Bake at 375°F for 20–25 minutes.
