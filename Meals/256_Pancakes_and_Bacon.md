### Pancakes and Bacon
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Pancake mix  
- Eggs  
- Milk  
- Butter  
- Bacon  
- Optional: maple syrup  

**Quick Prep:**  
Cook bacon in the oven at 400°F while mixing batter and cooking pancakes on a griddle until golden.
