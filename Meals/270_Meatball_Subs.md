### Meatball Subs
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Ground beef  
- Breadcrumbs  
- Egg  
- Marinara sauce  
- Sub rolls  
- Mozzarella cheese  
- Garlic  

**Quick Prep:**  
Mix beef with breadcrumbs, egg, and garlic, form meatballs, bake at 400°F for 15 minutes, then simmer in marinara and serve on rolls with melted mozzarella.

**Tags:** #beef #italian #comfortfood #30min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 30 min
