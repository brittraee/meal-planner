### Creamy Chicken Potato Skillet
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Chicken breast  
- Potatoes  
- Onion  
- Garlic  
- Shredded cheese  
- Milk or broth  

**Quick Prep:**  
Pan-fry potatoes and chicken. Add garlic, milk, and cheese for creamy finish.

**Tags:** #chicken #skillet #comfortfood #25min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 25 min
