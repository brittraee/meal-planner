### Ranch Chicken Sheet Pan Bake
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Chicken breast  
- Ranch seasoning  
- Baby potatoes or hash browns  
- Carrots or green beans  
- Shredded cheese (optional)  

**Quick Prep:**  
Toss ingredients with oil and bake at 400°F for 30–35 minutes.

**Tags:** #chicken #sheetpan #kidfriendly #30min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 30 min
