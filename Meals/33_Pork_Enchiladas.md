### Pork Enchiladas
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Shredded pork  
- Tortillas  
- Enchilada sauce  
- Cheese  
- Beans or corn (optional)  

**Quick Prep:**  
Roll pork and cheese in tortillas, top with sauce and more cheese. Bake at 375°F for 20–25 minutes.
