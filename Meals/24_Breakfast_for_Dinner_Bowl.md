### Breakfast-for-Dinner Bowl
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Eggs  
- Potatoes or hash browns  
- Cheese  
- Onion or bell pepper  
- Bacon, sausage, or ham (optional)  

**Quick Prep:**  
Scramble or fry eggs, cook with potatoes and veg. Top with cheese.

**Tags:** #brinner #eggs #comfortfood #20min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 20 min
