### Garlic Butter Shrimp with Rice or Veggies
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Shrimp  
- Garlic  
- Butter or olive oil  
- Lemon (optional)  
- Rice or frozen vegetables  

**Quick Prep:**  
Sauté shrimp with garlic and butter. Serve over rice or steam-in-bag veggies.

**Tags:** #shrimp #quickmeal #solodinner #lowspoon #20min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 20 min
