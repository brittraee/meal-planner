### Cheesy Chicken Broccoli Rice Bake
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Chicken breast  
- Jasmine rice  
- Frozen or fresh broccoli  
- Cream of chicken or mushroom soup  
- Shredded cheese  
- Crispy fried onions (optional)  

**Quick Prep:**  
Mix cooked chicken, rice, broccoli, soup, and cheese. Bake at 375°F for 25 minutes.

**Prep Time:** 15 min | **Total Time:** 40 min
