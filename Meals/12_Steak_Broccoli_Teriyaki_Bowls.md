### Steak & Broccoli Teriyaki Bowls
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Steaks  
- Jasmine rice  
- Broccoli (fresh or frozen)  
- Garlic  
- Soy sauce  
- Honey or brown sugar  

**Quick Prep:**  
Sear steak and broccoli. Mix garlic, soy sauce, and sweetener into glaze. Serve over rice.

**Tags:** #steak #teriyaki #ricebowl #quick #20min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 20 min
