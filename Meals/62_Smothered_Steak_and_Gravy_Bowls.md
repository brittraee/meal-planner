### Smothered Steak & Gravy Bowls
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Steak  
- Onion  
- Brown gravy mix or pan gravy  
- Mashed potatoes or rice  
- Green beans or broccoli  

**Quick Prep:**  
Pan-cook steak and onions. Make gravy and layer over starch and veggies.

**Tags:** #steak #comfortfood #batchfriendly #20min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 20 min
