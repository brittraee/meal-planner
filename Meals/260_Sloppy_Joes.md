### Sloppy Joes
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Ground beef  
- Onion  
- Ketchup  
- Brown sugar  
- Worcestershire sauce  
- Hamburger buns  

**Quick Prep:**  
Brown beef with onion, stir in ketchup, brown sugar, and Worcestershire, simmer 10 minutes, and serve on buns.

**Tags:** #beef #american #kidfriendly #quick #30min

**Prep Time:** 5 min | **Total Time:** 25 min
