### Pork Fried Rice
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Shredded pork  
- Cooked rice  
- Eggs  
- Frozen stir fry vegetables  
- Soy sauce  
- Garlic or green onion  

**Quick Prep:**  
Scramble egg, sauté pork and veg, stir in rice and soy sauce.

**Tags:** #pork #friedrice #onepan #fastdinner #20min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 20 min
