### Chicken and Biscuits
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Cooked chicken  
- Cream of chicken soup  
- Frozen vegetables  
- Refrigerated or drop biscuits  
- Shredded cheese (optional)  

**Quick Prep:**  
Mix chicken, soup, and veg. Top with biscuit dough. Bake until golden.

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 25 min
