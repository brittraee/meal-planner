### Steak & Pesto Flatbreads
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Cooked steak  
- Flatbreads or naan  
- Pesto  
- Mozzarella or shredded cheese  
- Tomato or red onion  

**Quick Prep:**  
Layer pesto, steak, and cheese on flatbreads. Bake or toast until melty.

**Tags:** #steak #flatbread #pesto #quickdinner #20min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 20 min

**Instructions:**  
Preheat oven to 425°. Place dough on a lightly floured surface; let rest for 10 minutes. Knead in 1/4 cup cheese, basil and the oregano. Roll into a 12-in. circle; place on a greased 14-in. pizza pan. Prick with a fork. Bake 10 minutes.
Spread pesto over crust. Sprinkle with mushrooms, mozzarella cheese and the remaining Parmesan cheese. Bake pizza until golden brown, 8-10 minutes longer.
