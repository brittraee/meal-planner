### Baked Cajun Cod with Cabbage & Potatoes
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Cod fillet  
- Cabbage  
- Potatoes  
- Garlic  
- Cajun seasoning  
- Apple or citrus  
- Scallions  

**Quick Prep:**  
Toss veg with oil and roast. Bake cod with seasoning on top. Serve over cabbage with citrus garnish.

**Tags:** #cajun #cod #sheetpan #whole30 #30min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 30 min
