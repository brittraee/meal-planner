### Shrimp Quesadilla
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Shrimp  
- Tortilla  
- Shredded cheese  
- Garlic or chili powder  
- Salsa or hot sauce (optional)  

**Quick Prep:**  
Cook shrimp with seasoning. Fill tortilla with cheese + shrimp, pan-fry until crisp.

**Tags:** #shrimp #quesadilla #snackdinner #easy #20min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 20 min
