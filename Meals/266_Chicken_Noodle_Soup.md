### Chicken Noodle Soup
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Chicken breast  
- Egg noodles  
- Carrots  
- Celery  
- Onion  
- Chicken broth  
- Garlic  

**Quick Prep:**  
Simmer chicken in broth with vegetables until cooked, shred chicken, add noodles and cook until tender.

**Tags:** #chicken #american #comfortfood #45min

**Prep Time:** 15 min | **Total Time:** 45 min
