### Philly Cheesesteak
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Ribeye or sirloin steak  
- Hoagie rolls  
- Provolone cheese  
- Onion  
- Bell pepper  
- Optional: mushrooms  

**Quick Prep:**  
Thinly slice steak, sear in a hot skillet, saute peppers and onions, pile onto rolls and top with melted provolone.

**Tags:** #beef #american #comfortfood #30min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 25 min
