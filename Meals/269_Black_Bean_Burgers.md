### Black Bean Burgers
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Black beans (canned)  
- Breadcrumbs  
- Eggs  
- Onion  
- Garlic  
- Cumin  
- Hamburger buns  

**Quick Prep:**  
Mash beans, mix with breadcrumbs, egg, onion, garlic, and cumin, form patties, and pan-fry until crispy on each side.

**Tags:** #vegetarian #american #healthy #30min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 25 min
