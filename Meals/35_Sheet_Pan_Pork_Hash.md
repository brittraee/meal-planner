### Sheet Pan Pork Hash
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Shredded pork  
- Potatoes  
- Bell peppers  
- Onion  
- Olive oil  
- Eggs (optional)  

**Quick Prep:**  
Toss all ingredients on sheet pan with oil. Roast at 425°F for 25–30 minutes.
