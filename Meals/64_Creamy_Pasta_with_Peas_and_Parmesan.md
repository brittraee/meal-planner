### Creamy Pasta with Peas & Parmesan
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Pasta  
- Frozen peas  
- Garlic  
- Parmesan cheese  
- Cream or milk  
- Butter  

**Quick Prep:**  
Boil pasta and peas. Make quick cream sauce with garlic and cheese. Mix and serve.

**Tags:** #pasta #vegetarian #batchfriendly #freezable #25min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 25 min
