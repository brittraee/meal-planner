### Chili
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Ground beef or turkey  
- Onion  
- Canned beans  
- Diced tomatoes  
- Chili seasoning  
- Optional: bell pepper or corn  

**Quick Prep:**  
Brown meat and onion. Add remaining ingredients. Simmer until thick.

**Prep Time:** 15 min | **Total Time:** 45 min
