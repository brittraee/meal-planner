### Creole Fish Cakes with Corn Succotash
**Main Ingredients:**  
- White fish fillets (tilapia or pollock)  
- Poblano pepper  
- Corn  
- Red bell pepper  
- Green onions  
- Chipotle crema or sour cream + chipotle seasoning  
- Panko breadcrumbs  

**Quick Prep:**  
Mix fish with seasoning and panko to form cakes. Pan-fry. Serve over sautéed succotash.

**Tags:** #fish #succotash #chipotle #creole #dinner #35min

**Prep Time:** 15 min | **Total Time:** 35 min
