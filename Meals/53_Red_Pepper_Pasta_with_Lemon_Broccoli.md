### Roasted Red Pepper Pasta with Lemon Broccoli
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Pasta  
- Broccoli  
- Garlic  
- Roasted red peppers or piquillo peppers  
- Tomato paste  
- Parmesan  
- Capers  
- Cream or half & half  

**Quick Prep:**  
Sauté garlic, peppers, and tomato paste. Add cream and capers for sauce. Toss with pasta and broccoli.

**Tags:** #pasta #redpepper #vegetarian #pantryfriendly #25min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 25 min
