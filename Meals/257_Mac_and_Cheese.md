### Mac and Cheese
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Elbow macaroni  
- Butter  
- Flour  
- Milk  
- Sharp cheddar cheese  
- Optional: breadcrumbs  

**Quick Prep:**  
Cook pasta, make a roux with butter and flour, stir in milk and cheese until smooth, then toss with pasta.

**Tags:** #vegetarian #american #comfortfood #kidfriendly #30min

**Prep Time:** 5 min | **Total Time:** 25 min
