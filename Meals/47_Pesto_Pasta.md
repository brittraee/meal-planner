### Pesto Pasta
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Pasta  
- Pesto  
- Frozen peas or broccoli  
- Parmesan cheese  
- Optional: cherry tomatoes or pine nuts  

**Quick Prep:**  
Cook pasta and veg. Mix with pesto and cheese.

**Tags:** #pasta #pesto #staple #easy #25min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 25 min
