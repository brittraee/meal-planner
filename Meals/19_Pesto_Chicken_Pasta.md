### Pesto Chicken Pasta
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Chicken breast  
- Pasta  
- Pesto  
- Frozen peas or spinach  
- Parmesan cheese  

**Quick Prep:**  
Cook pasta + peas. Toss with pesto and chicken. Sprinkle with Parmesan.

**Tags:** #chicken #pasta #easy #familymeal #25min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 25 min
