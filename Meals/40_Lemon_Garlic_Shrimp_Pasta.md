### Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Shrimp  
- Pasta  
- Garlic  
- Olive oil or butter  
- Lemon  
- Parmesan cheese (optional)  

**Quick Prep:**  
Sauté shrimp in garlic and lemon oil. Toss with cooked pasta and cheese.

**Tags:** #shrimp #pasta #solomeal #quick #25min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 25 min
