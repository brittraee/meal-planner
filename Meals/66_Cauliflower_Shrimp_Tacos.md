### Cauliflower Shrimp Tacos
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Shrimp  
- Riced cauliflower  
- Taco seasoning  
- Tortillas  
- Cabbage slaw or lettuce  
- Lime or crema  

**Quick Prep:**  
Cook shrimp and riced cauliflower with seasoning. Assemble tacos with slaw and sauce.

**Tags:** #shrimp #tacos #lowcarb #weeknight #25min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 25 min
