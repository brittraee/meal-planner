### Orange-Chile Glazed Chicken (Double Batch)
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Chicken breast  
- Orange juice  
- Chili powder  
- Garlic  
- Broccoli  
- Potatoes  
- Carrots  
- Green onions  

**Quick Prep:**  
Glaze chicken with orange-chili sauce. Roast or steam veggies. Serve with mashed or roasted potatoes.

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 30 min
