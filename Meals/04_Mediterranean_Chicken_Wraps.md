### Mediterranean Chicken Wraps
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Chicken breast  
- Pita or naan  
- Cucumber  
- Cherry tomatoes  
- Hummus or tzatziki  
- Feta cheese  
- Red onion  

**Quick Prep:**  
Cook seasoned chicken and slice. Assemble wraps with toppings.
