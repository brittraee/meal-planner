### Chile & Orange-Glazed Chicken
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Chicken breast  
- Orange juice  
- Chili powder or ancho chile paste  
- Garlic  
- Potatoes  
- Carrots  
- Broccoli  
- Green onions  

**Quick Prep:**  
Glaze chicken in orange juice + chili mix. Serve with mashed potatoes and roasted veggies.

**Tags:** #chicken #glazed #roastedveg #sheetpan #30min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 30 min
