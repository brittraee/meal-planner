### Pork & Veggie Stir Fry
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Shredded pork  
- Stir fry vegetables  
- Garlic or onion  
- Soy sauce  
- Cooked rice or noodles  

**Quick Prep:**  
Sauté pork and vegetables with garlic and soy sauce. Serve over rice or noodles.

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 25 min
