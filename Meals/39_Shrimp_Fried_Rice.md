### Shrimp Fried Rice
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Shrimp  
- Cooked rice  
- Egg  
- Frozen vegetables  
- Soy sauce  
- Green onion (optional)  

**Quick Prep:**  
Scramble egg, sauté shrimp and veg, stir in rice and soy sauce.

**Tags:** #shrimp #friedrice #onepan #leftovers #25min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 25 min
