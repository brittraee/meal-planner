### French Toast
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Bread (thick-sliced)  
- Eggs  
- Milk  
- Cinnamon  
- Butter  
- Optional: maple syrup  

**Quick Prep:**  
Whisk eggs, milk, and cinnamon, dip bread slices, and cook on a buttered griddle until golden on both sides.

**Tags:** #vegetarian #american #breakfastfordinner #kidfriendly #20min

**Prep Time:** 5 min | **Total Time:** 20 min
