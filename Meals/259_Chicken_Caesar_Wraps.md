### Chicken Caesar Wraps
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Chicken breast  
- Flour tortillas  
- Romaine lettuce  
- Parmesan cheese  
- Caesar dressing  
- Optional: croutons  

**Quick Prep:**  
Season and cook chicken, slice thin, then wrap with romaine, parmesan, and Caesar dressing in tortillas.

**Tags:** #chicken #american #quick #30min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 25 min
