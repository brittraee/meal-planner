### Beef Tacos
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Ground beef  
- Taco shells  
- Taco seasoning  
- Shredded cheese  
- Lettuce  
- Tomato  
- Optional: sour cream  

**Quick Prep:**  
Brown beef with taco seasoning, warm shells, and set out toppings for everyone to build their own tacos.

**Tags:** #beef #mexican #quick #kidfriendly #25min

**Prep Time:** 5 min | **Total Time:** 20 min
