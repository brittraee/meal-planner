### Chicken Broccoli Alfredo
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Chicken breast  
- Pasta  
- Broccoli  
- Garlic  
- Heavy cream or milk  
- Parmesan cheese  

**Quick Prep:**  
Cook pasta and broccoli. Make Alfredo sauce with garlic, cream, and cheese. Combine with chicken.

**Tags:** #chicken #alfredo #staple #comfortfood #25min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 25 min
