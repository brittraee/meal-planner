### Lemon Herb Chicken Roast
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Chicken breast  
- Lemon  
- Parsley or dried herbs  
- Potatoes  
- Green beans  
- Dijon mustard (optional)  

**Quick Prep:**  
Marinate chicken. Roast with veggies at 425°F for 25 minutes.

**Tags:** #chicken #sheetpan #easy #30min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 30 min
