### Skillet Pesto Chicken
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Chicken breast  
- Pesto  
- Cherry tomatoes  
- Mozzarella or parmesan  
- Pasta or rice (optional)  

**Quick Prep:**  
Sear chicken, top with pesto, tomatoes, and cheese. Serve as-is or with pasta.

**Tags:** #chicken #pesto #skillet #quick #25min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 25 min
