### Chicken Alfredo Pasta
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Chicken breast  
- Pasta  
- Parmesan cheese  
- Heavy cream or milk  
- Garlic  

**Quick Prep:**  
Cook pasta and chicken. Simmer garlic, cream, and cheese for sauce. Mix together.

**Tags:** #chicken #pasta #comfortfood #25min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 25 min
