### Ranch Chicken Potato Bake
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Chicken breast  
- Ranch seasoning  
- Potatoes  
- Carrots or green beans  
- Cheese (optional)  

**Quick Prep:**  
Toss everything with oil and seasoning. Bake at 400°F for 30–35 minutes.

**Tags:** #chicken #sheetpan #ranch #easy #30min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 30 min
