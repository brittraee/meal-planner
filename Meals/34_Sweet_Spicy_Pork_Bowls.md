### Sweet & Spicy Pork Rice Bowls
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Shredded pork  
- Cooked rice  
- Soy sauce  
- Brown sugar or honey  
- Sriracha or chili sauce  
- Cucumber or carrot (optional)  

**Quick Prep:**  
Mix pork with glaze. Serve over rice with optional fresh veggie topping.
