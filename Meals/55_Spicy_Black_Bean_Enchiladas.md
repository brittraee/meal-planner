### Spicy Black Bean Enchiladas
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Black beans  
- Rice  
- Tortillas  
- Chipotle chili paste or seasoning  
- Cheese (Monterey Jack)  
- Mexican crema or sour cream  
- Tomatoes  
- Lime  

**Quick Prep:**  
Mix beans, rice, tomatoes. Roll into tortillas, top with chipotle sauce and cheese. Bake and serve with crema.

**Tags:** #enchiladas #vegetarian #comfortfood #bake #40min

**Prep Time:** 15 min | **Total Time:** 40 min
