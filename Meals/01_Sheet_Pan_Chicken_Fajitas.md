### Sheet Pan Chicken Fajitas
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Chicken breast  
- Bell peppers  
- Onion  
- Fajita or taco seasoning  
- Tortillas  
- Sour cream or salsa (optional)  

**Quick Prep:**  
Slice everything, toss in oil + seasoning, and roast at 425°F for 20–25 minutes.

**Tags:** #chicken #sheetpan #kidfriendly #30min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 30 min
