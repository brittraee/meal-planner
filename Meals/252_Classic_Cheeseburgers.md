### Classic Cheeseburgers
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Ground beef  
- Hamburger buns  
- American cheese  
- Lettuce  
- Tomato  
- Onion  
- Optional: pickles  

**Quick Prep:**  
Form beef into patties, season with salt and pepper, grill or pan-sear 4 minutes per side, and top with cheese to melt.

**Tags:** #beef #american #quick #kidfriendly #30min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 25 min
