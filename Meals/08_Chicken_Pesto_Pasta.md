### Chicken Pesto Pasta with Peas
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Chicken breast  
- Pasta  
- Pesto  
- Frozen peas  
- Parmesan cheese  

**Quick Prep:**  
Cook pasta and peas. Toss with pesto and chicken. Top with cheese.

**Tags:** #chicken #pasta #quick #25min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 25 min
