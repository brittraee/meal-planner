### Shrimp Tortellini Scampi
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Shrimp  
- Tortellini (refrigerated or frozen)  
- Garlic  
- Butter or olive oil  
- Parmesan  
- Lemon  

**Quick Prep:**  
Boil tortellini. Sauté shrimp in garlic butter. Toss all with lemon and cheese.

**Tags:** #shrimp #pasta #scampi #easy #25min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 25 min
