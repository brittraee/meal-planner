### Mediterranean Chicken Bowl
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Chicken breast  
- Cooked rice or quinoa  
- Cucumber  
- Cherry tomatoes  
- Feta cheese  
- Hummus or tzatziki  
- Red onion  

**Quick Prep:**  
Cook and slice chicken. Layer with toppings over rice or greens.

**Tags:** #chicken #bowl #mediterranean #fresh #20min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 20 min
