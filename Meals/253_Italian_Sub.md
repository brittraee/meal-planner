### Italian Sub
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Italian deli meat (salami, capicola, ham)  
- Sub rolls  
- Provolone cheese  
- Lettuce  
- Tomato  
- Red onion  
- Italian dressing  

**Quick Prep:**  
Layer deli meats and provolone on sub rolls, top with lettuce, tomato, onion, and a drizzle of Italian dressing.

**Tags:** #pork #italian #quick #30min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 10 min
