### BBQ Chicken Rice Bowl
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Cooked chicken  
- BBQ sauce  
- Cooked rice  
- Corn  
- Shredded cheese  
- Green onion (optional)  

**Quick Prep:**  
Warm chicken with BBQ sauce. Serve over rice with toppings.

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 20 min
