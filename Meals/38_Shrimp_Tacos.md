### Shrimp Tacos
**Main Ingredients:**  
- Shrimp  
- Taco seasoning  
- Tortillas  
- Coleslaw mix or shredded lettuce  
- Sour cream or lime crema  

**Quick Prep:**  
Sear shrimp with taco seasoning. Serve in tortillas with slaw and a drizzle.

**Tags:** #shrimp #tacos #fastdinner #lightmeal #20min

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Total Time:** 20 min
